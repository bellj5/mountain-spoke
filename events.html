Featured Event: Cycle the Continental Divide

What

We are proud to announce that we are an avid sponsor of the internationally renowned event: "Cycle the Continental Divide." We are calling determined cyclists everywhere to join us in this extraordinary event to cycle from Alberta, Canada through British Columbia, Montana, Idaho, Wyoming, Colorado, and New Mexico. By the time the event is over, participants will have climbed almost 200,000 feet. This is roughly the same as climbing Mount Everest from sea-level seven times!

Requirements and Dates

Cyclists that have met the requirements found at the Official Tour Guide can sign up by June 10th of each year to take place in this amazing event!
